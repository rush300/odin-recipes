Ingredients

  • 200 g guanciale or pancetta, cubed
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2 whole eggs

Steps

  1. Step 1: Heat a wide pan over a medium low heat, drop in the guanciale or pancetta and let it cook gently until all the fat has rendered. Once the fat is rendered, turn the heat off.
  2. Step 2: Whisk the egg yolks, whole eggs, pinch of salt and pepper with 3/4 of the parmesan and set aside.
